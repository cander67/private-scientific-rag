# Deferred Patent Bulk Data

Patent coverage in PRD3 is limited to user-uploaded PDFs. Bulk downloads, raw patent-data feeds, archive provenance, and multi-jurisdiction patent parsing are deferred to `prds/12-bulk-patent-data-integration.md`.

Candidate tools to revisit:

- `patent_client` Python library for application-owned ingestion adapters.
- `patent-client-agents` for research and MCP-assisted exploration.
- USPTO Open Data Portal and Bulk Data Storage System for authoritative US source data.
