Page 4 · Section 3 — Methods

Zeolite membranes were synthesized by secondary (seeded) growth on porous α-Al₂O₃ supports. Seed crystals were prepared and deposited prior to hydrothermal treatment.

chunk 12 · p.4 · Methods · offsets 4120–4498
The synthesis solution had a molar composition of 100 SiO₂ : 5 Al₂O₃ : 50 Na₂O : 4000 H₂O. Hydrothermal crystallization was carried out at 448 K for 24 h. After synthesis, membranes were calcined at 773 K to remove the structure-directing agent.

Permeance and separation factors were measured using a Wicke–Kallenbach setup with an equimolar CO₂/CH₄ feed at 308 K, as summarized in Table 2.
