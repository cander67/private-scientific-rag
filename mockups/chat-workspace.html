What CO₂/CH₄ selectivity has been reported for SSZ-13 membranes?
Reported CO₂/CH₄ separation factors for SSZ-13 membranes range from roughly 42 to 58 at 308 K , with CO₂ permeance near 1.1 × 10⁻⁷ mol·m⁻²·s⁻¹·Pa⁻¹ . A related patent example reports selectivity above 50 under mixed-gas feed .

The repository does not contain long-term stability data for these membranes.
How was selectivity measured?
Measurements used a Wicke–Kallenbach setup with an equimolar CO₂/CH₄ feed at 308 K .
